Chocolate Cake

This chocolate cake is soft, rich, and very easy to make at home.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. Mix all dry ingredients.
  3. Add eggs and milk.
  4. Stir until smooth.
  5. Bake for 30 minutes.
